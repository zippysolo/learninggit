How to make delicious Chocolate Chip Cookies

This is an easy one-bowl method

Ingredients

2 eggs
3/4 c oil
1 vanilla sugar
2 1/4 cups flour
1/4 tsp salt
1 tsp baking powder
1 cup chocolate chips

Method

Preheat oven to 180 C

Combine oil, sugar, eggs and vanilla in a bowl and mix until fluffy

Add flour, salt and baking powder and stir well

Add chocolate chips

Roll into balls on oven tray.

Bake for 10 minutes.
